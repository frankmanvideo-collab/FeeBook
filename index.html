Why Tuition Tutors Trust FeeBook 📚

💵 Track Partial Cash Installments

Most tuition teachers in India collect fees in cash, often in multiple installments (e.g. ₹500 today, ₹500 next week). FeeBook lets you log a partial payment and track the remaining balance effortlessly. No more messy paper registers.

💬 Zero-Storage WhatsApp Sharing

Generate clean, professional payment receipts, marked attendance, and homework sheets. Share them directly on WhatsApp with parents. Since we use your device's native sharing, no heavy files are uploaded to our database, keeping it 100% free and lightning-fast.

📝 Shared Homework & PDF Worksheets

Assign daily homework with subjects, topics, and direct links. You can attach reference images or question papers as PDFs to share directly on parents' WhatsApp chats in one tap.

📅 Smart Attendance & Holidays

Mark attendance with present, absent, or late statuses. FeeBook automatically handles custom working days to help you calculate active teaching sessions and manage multiple student batches.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is FeeBook completely free for tuition teachers?

Yes! FeeBook is free forever for managing up to 25 active students with all standard features included. Perfect for individual home tutors and small coaching batches.

Q: How do I upgrade to the Pro plan?

For managing unlimited students, creating extra batches, and exporting reports, you can upgrade to FeeBook Pro for just ₹99/month. Simply contact us at [email protected] for manual activation.

Q: Is my student and financial data secure?

Absolutely. FeeBook is built on Supabase, an enterprise-grade backend with secure PostgreSQL Row-Level Security (RLS). Only you can view or edit your student records and payment data.

Q: Does the application work offline?

Yes, FeeBook is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It stores your session and logs locally on your phone, syncing automatically to the database when your internet connection is active.
