Features

Built Specifically for Indian Tuition Tutors

No complex corporate billing. We understand exactly how tuition batches, cash collections, and parent coordination work in India.

💵

Track Partial Cash Installments

Most tuition teachers collect fees in cash, often in multiple small installments (e.g. ₹500 today, ₹500 next week). FeeBook lets you log partial cash collections and tracks the remaining student balances automatically. No more paper registers.

💬

Zero-Storage WhatsApp Share

Generate clean, professional cash/online receipt cards. Share them directly on WhatsApp to parents' numbers. Since we use your device's native sharing capabilities, no photos are uploaded to our database, keeping the app 100% free and lightning-fast.

📎

Homework & PDF Sharing

Assign daily homework with clean text, topics, and references. Select and attach worksheets as PDFs or snaps of your blackboard directly to share on WhatsApp in one single click, without consuming any cloud storage.

📅

Attendance & Holiday Calendar

Mark student attendance (present, absent, late) with custom teaching days. The calendar automatically factors in standard Indian holidays to help you calculate active sessions and track student regularity.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is FeeBook really free?

Yes! FeeBook is 100% free for managing up to 25 active students. You can log unlimited payments, mark attendance, and share receipts without any limits.

How do I upgrade to the Pro plan?

Because the developer is currently in the 11th grade, we process Pro activations manually. Just email us at **[email protected]** or send a WhatsApp to **+91 9973898852**, and we will activate your unlimited plan via a direct code within minutes!

Does the application work offline?

Yes, FeeBook is a Progressive Web App (PWA). It stores your sessions and logs locally on your phone, syncing automatically to the secure database whenever you connect to the internet.

How is my financial and student data protected?

All data is hosted securely on Supabase using encrypted PostgreSQL servers. We employ strict Row-Level Security (RLS) policies—this means only you can access or modify your logged student and fee details.
